The Natural Gas Compressor, Parts and Service Supplier across Canada

Gateway Compression Inc. was formed with an exclusive distributorship with Universal Starters Inc. (Austart) , CECO polymer products and Knox Western natural gas compressors.

Gateway Compression supplies “Austart” air/gas starters for natural gas or diesel engine applications. See the Austart starter tab for more information and to compare advantages of Austart over the competition.

Gateway Compression is the Western Canadian distributor for “CECO” Compressor products. Gateway stocks a variety of rod packing, wiper rings, piston rings, and rider bands in a variety of proprietary material configurations. For more product information visit the CECO tab.

Gateway Compression carries a large inventory of repair parts for the Knox Western compressor as well as new and exchange compressor frames. Visit the Knox Western tab for more information on all your compressor requirements.
